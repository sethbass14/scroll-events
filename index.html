Scroll Definition

Noun

  • 1. a roll of parchment or paper for writing or painting on.
  • 2. the facility that moves a display on a computer screen in order to view new material.

Verbs

  • 1. move displayed text or graphics in a particular direction on a computer screen in order to view different parts of them.
  • 2. cause to move like paper rolling or unrolling.
